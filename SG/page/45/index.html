89.4

89.4
The gallery has been set up in a massive room with intricate
tile floors. The art itself is displayed on a variety of wooden
easels throughout the room. Guests wander through, scrutinizing
the work and debating its merits.

Ed stands up straighter, clearly comfortable in this environment.
“It almost feels like I’m back home,” he says. “Although
I rarely have such a nice space to display the pieces I have up
for sale.”

Miguel ignores him, looking around with a purpose until he
spots what he’s looking for. “Ed! There!”

Miguel points to a small piece off to one side. Few people are
bothering to look at it. It’s not one of the showiest pieces in
the collection, but as soon as you see it you can understand
why it’s important to Ed and Miguel.

Woman with Hairpin is an oil painting depicting a young Chinese
woman in traditional clothing. The silver pin in her hair
prominently features a large ruby gemstone. Ed approaches
the painting, a far-off look in his eyes. “Ruby’s mother loved
this painting.”

A. Bid on Woman with Hairpin.
Pay 6 resources. If you can’t or don’t pay, you must choose B.
(Turn to 89.6)

B. Hide in the gallery and wait until everyone has gone home.

89.6

89.6
When you return the next day to retrieve the painting, Tekla
comes to congratulate you on winning the bid for Woman with
Hairpin. To your surprise he looks less like a thief and more
like a respectable businessman-someone whom Ed may have
considered a peer in different circumstances.

“The Milius Miners and Merchants Concern thanks you for
your support,” says Tekla. “We do hope you’ll enjoy your new
painting.”

“Thank you,” says Ed, shaking Tekla’s hand. And then, more
quietly so that you can barely hear it, Ed adds, “If you ever
steal from me again, I won’t let you off the hook so easily.”

With the painting back in his possession, Ed looks both
relieved and sad.

Miguel puts a comforting hand on his shoulder. “Don’t worry.
We’ll see her again. And I’m sure she’ll be glad you changed
your mind about selling the painting.”

Ed smiles a little sadly. “Thank you. I suppose you might not
turn out to be the worst son-in-law in the world after all.”

Complete quest 160. Gain adventure card 118 (Woman with Hairpin).
Gain 2 fire quartz. Cross off location 89. Return to the map.

90~
“Wait a moment.” Ezarius bends down to lift an ordinary-looking
rock by an ordinary-looking tree to reveal a sealed envelope
underneath. “I have allies who leave messages for me
here.” He is distracted, scanning the letter with hungry eyes.
As he reads, his expression darkens.

Jessie pats him on the back. “You feeling okay, buddy?”

Ezarius shakes his head. “Pran is being threatened by Orfashian
cultists, cursed fools obsessed with violence and
conquest. We took refuge in Pran after making some bitter
enemies of the gods. No one was supposed to be able to find
us. Even without their powers, the gods can’t just let us live.”

Ezarius turns towards the south, his expression full of anger,
fear, and longing.

He turns back towards you. “Please. It’s out of your way, and
it’s no benefit to you, but I’ll never make it on time on my own.
If you help me rescue Pran, I will be in your debt.”

“Of course, we’re going to help! Right, Claire?” Jessie shoots
you a glance that says you’d better not argue.

Gain quests 161 and 162. Cross off location 90. Return to the map.

91.1

91.1
A small camp is nestled in a thick patch of woodland, with
canvas tents of brown and green to blend into the environment.
If you hadn’t known where to look, you might have
never spotted it.

“Arlos, set up the drying rack over there, by the fire pit.” The
man issuing orders is tall, but thin, with warm dark skin and
long locks that reach halfway down his back. His demeanor
is confident, but not demanding, and the others follow his
orders gladly.

“Zenig!”

The man turns and his face lights up as he spots Ezarius.
Ezarius runs up to him and the two men embrace.

“Thank the gods you’re okay!”

Zenig laughs. “I never thought I’d hear you thank the gods for
anything.”

Ezarius looks embarrassed. “It’s merely an expression.”

“Who are these people with you?” Zenig looks at you with
curiosity, but not hostility. “They look like other-worlders.”

Ezarius nods. “Zenig, these are my colleagues in the work I
am doing with Meecra. And everyone, this is Zenig Nel, my
husband.”

“Your husband?” Jessie seems delighted. “Why didn’t you tell
us earlier we were looking for someone so important?”

Ezarius shuffles his feet, still embarrassed. “I am sorry for my
secrecy, but we have many enemies, and over the years I’ve
learned not to trust easily. But you’ve proven yourselves to be
true allies-friends, even. Thank you all for your help. I can’t
tell you how much it means to me to know that Zenig is safe.’’

Zenig shakes your hand. “I’m so pleased to meet you all. We
lost a lot in our fight, so we can’t offer you much, but I managed
to steal this from the cultists during the battle. Maybe
it will help you. And you’re welcome back here any time you
need a bit of rest and rejuvenation on your journey. We’re
probably going to be staying here for a while.”

Complete quest 163. Gain quests 164 and 165. Restore 3 health
and remove 1 status effect. Return to the map.
