Flame sputters from the ground, bursting into a cloud of acrid
smoke. An enormous figure emerges-a bull-man of earth and
ash. Crusty blood on a dark hide surrounds scorched eyes of
wild flame below burning horns so wide and powerful they
could crush a freight car.

When he speaks, the air ripples with heat and anger. “So
you have chosen to serve Meecra. She is a weak god and a
deceiver. I will destroy her, and you will suffer in pain for many
days before the end.”

Ezarius steps forward boldly. “You do not frighten us, Orfash.
You make a show of power, but you are weak. Even now, you
dare not attack us.”

Orfash stamps the ground in fury. “Silence, Lukran! I would
kill you where you stand, but a slow vengeance is more satisfying.”

He draws a leather whip and strikes your neck, leaving a
painful, smoking gash. With a harsh laugh, he disappears in a
flash of smoke.

Pain shoots through your neck and strange visions fill your
head. You see the face of your father, stern and angry. In
another flash, your plane crashes. You find yourself trapped
in a stately bedroom: cold, empty, and doorless. Bars cover
the windows and block your way out. You call out, but your
breath is gone and your lungs burn.

Ezarius grips your hand and the visions disappear.

“Claire! Are you alright?” Jessie holds you steady as Ezarius
examines the wound.

“Meecra! We need you!” says Miguel, but the goddess does
not appear.

“I was hallucinating,” you say, gritting your teeth as the striking
pain runs up your neck. “It was like a nightmare.”

Ezarius nods, a grim certainty in his eyes. “Then this wound
is likely a powerful curse, one that preys upon your fears and
will deter you from your path. We must tell Meecra.”

As you wait for the pain to subside, Miguel scouts the area,
returning with a satchel filled with maps. “Perhaps this belonged
to some of Orfash’s followers.”

Complete quest 128. Gain quests 129, 138, and 207. Shuffle Orfash’s
Curse cards into the ability deck (including the ability discard
pile), depending on the game difficulty: easy (shuffle in curse cards
1-3), normal (shuffle in curse cards 1-4), hard (shuffle in curse
cards 1-5). Whenever you draw an Orfash’s Curse card, apply the
effect and draw again immediately. Return to the map.

The one-eyed fish writhes on the ground for a moment before
coughing up a jeweled goblet. A moment later, the fish
crumbles into dust. You quickly scoop up the goblet, feeling
the foundations of the castle beginning to rumble underneath
you.

Ezarius reaches out a hand to steady himself against a wall.
“The power of the goblet must have been sustaining this
ghost of a place!” he says. “Without it, the castle will crumble.”

Jessie curses as another wave of tremors nearly knocks her off
her feet. “In that case, let’s beat it before we get caught in the
crush!”

You grab what you can on your way out, barely making it to
the surface before the tunnel falls apart and seals itself behind
you. You’re relieved to be back on the surface, but not everyone
on your crew feels the same.

Complete quest 172. Gain quest 204. Gain adventure card 121
(Divine Goblet). Gain 3 ice diamonds, 2 jadeworm eggs, 1 torch,
and 1 rope. Cross off location 94. Return to the map.

into a woman made of mud.

“I am the Seer of Mud,” she says. “What have you brought for

102.1

102.1
The pilgrims give the Seer of Mud a bag of herbs.

“These are common to me,” says the mud woman. “I cannot
share wisdom with so little inspiration.”

“But we journeyed so far!” says one of the pilgrims.

“Fine. Here is a little wisdom. Let your daughter make her own
choices. With the gods weakened, you now have a chance
for real freedom. Do not bind the hands of those you love, for
love will turn cold.”

The pilgrims bow to the mud woman but look unsatisfied.
They leave, mumbling to themselves.

The mud woman turns to you. “For you, I offer a vision without
an offering. I see you are from a distant world. You seek
the totems of the gods, and there is one lost in a red desert to
the south. You’ll find it beneath the blind eye.”

A sudden vision fills your mind. A bird flies from this cave,
soaring over mountains to the south, until it lands on a dead
tree in rocky desert filled with red cliffs.

The woman descends into the muddy pool until all that remains
are ripples.

Gain quest 83. Return to the map.

103.1
The shattered buildings must have once been magnificent,
but intricate columns and broken statues are all that’s left of a
great palace.

A bit of smoke drifts at the edge of your vision. Is there a fire
nearby?

“Did you see that?” asks Miguel.

“This place is thick with spirits,” says Ezarius, his shoulders
tensed.

More smoke drifts through the hall. It gathers together and
forms into headless people in tattered clothes, ethereal and
flowing as if underwater. Their heads suddenly appear:
cracked, floating insect heads.

“Disgusting,” says Jessie, backing away as the ghosts surround
you.
