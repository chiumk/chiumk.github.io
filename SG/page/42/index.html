83.6

83.6
As the wolf collapses in the snow, leaking a pond of crimson,
someone appears on the far side of the snowbank.

Recognition spans Orlig’s face. “My brother! We found you!”

Orlig’s brother stumbles down the snow toward the wolf.
“What have you done? You killed him!”

Orlig looks confused. “Yes, we killed the beast! And you’re
alive! What’s wrong?”

Orlig’s brother stumbles to the ground, gripping the wolf’s
giant head. “This wolf and I were friends. He kept me alive all
these weeks when I was alone. He helped me hunt. And now
you killed him.”

Orlig sinks. “But he seemed so dangerous.”

Marching back toward camp, Orlig’s brother is silent, his features
twisted in grief. You realize there’s nothing you can say,
so you bid farewell to the remaining hunters, who give you a
pouch of rare herbs.

Complete quest 56. Gain 2 tundra berries, 1 bloodflower, and 1
gergon leaf. Return to the map.

Pinewood smoke drifts up from a camp of scarlet tents. People
are busy cooking cuts of meat, sharpening blades, and stuffing
packs with rope and tools.

A Lukran woman with two swords on her belt introduces
herself as Marani. “Are you Red Heretics? Have you come to
join the mission?”

Ezarius answers before anyone can say a word. “No, we’re
simple travelers. But could you use some help here?”

The woman looks Ezarius up and down. “I suppose. We’re
short some of our crew. They got sick and had to stay at
Stormlock city, but we need a few more hands.”

Ed glances at a pile of weapons leaning against a canvas tent
