You’re nearly at the top when you lose your focus, your mind
wandering to fears about the Helthark and never making it
home. You grasp a rock before checking if it’s secure. It slips
from your fingers and you reach for help. No one can save
you. There’s nothing for you to do but fall.

When you wake, Ezarius stands above you, studying your
face. “I guess some gods are still merciful,” he says.

“It is a miracle,” says Miguel.

“Oh, God! Claire!” Jessie smothers you in a sobbing embrace.

“Are you alright?” you ask.

“We weren’t,” Ed says. He explains how it wasn’t just you
who fell from the falls. How you all woke on the rocks below,
covered in blood, but still alive.

“It’s the stone,” says Ezarius. “It’s bloodstone. I should have
recognized it. Bleed upon the stone, and the stone will give
you healing. It can’t cure every wound, of course,” he says
as he winces from aching ribs. “But it was enough to save us
from a certain death.”
