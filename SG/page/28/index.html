inside, but Gelca demands you hand them over.

“Don’t worry. I’ll make sure they’re used correctly,” says Ezarius,

You say goodbye and leave Laikeia with her aunt. Gelca

right choice in letting you take the totems.

“What did you tell her?” asks Jessie.

“It helped us get what we wanted. That’s all that matters.”
Ezarius strides ahead of the group, avoiding further conversation.
Ed and Jessie look at you, unconvinced, but you keep
silent. There’s something more Ezarius isn’t telling you.

40 (Laikeia). Gain 2 tundra berries and 1 gergon leaf. Return to the
map.

55.1
Miguel hunches in the snow, resting after the effort.

“Ed, what you said about Ruby … “ he starts. “I don’t know.
We had an argument before I left, and I’m worried she doesn’t
want me back.”

Ed is stern but sympathetic, “A father knows his daughter, Miguel.
She’ll always want you back. But it’s good you’re worried.
It means you won’t take her for granted!”

Miguel can’t hide the smile at the corner of his mouth. “I’ll
always be good to her; I promise. I’ll be the best son-in-law … “

Ed cringes. “Don’t make me regret telling you.”

Gain quest 105. Draw 2 ability cards. Cross off location 55. Return
to the map.
