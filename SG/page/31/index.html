60.14

60.14
You resist the necklace. George tries to grab it but you hold
him back.

“Destroy that thing! Quickly!” says Ed.

Jessie takes out her baseball bat and slams the necklace with
a roar, over and over, like she’s hitting a roach that just won’t
die. She stumbles back, shaking her head; the necklace is
unharmed.

You can feel the necklace speaking to you, drawing you
closer, coaxing you to hold it and claim it for yourself. As you
grasp for the chain, Ed snatches it and tosses it down a deep
chasm.

The spell on your mind falls away as the necklace tumbles into
darkness. “How did you throw it? I couldn’t think clearly,” you
say to him.

“My inner demons already have enough to say; there isn’t
much room for anything else,” says Ed with a wry grin.

“Thanks folks. Don’t know what came over me,” says George,
rubbing blood from where the necklace scraped his forehead.
You watch him edge toward the chasm, looking down with
longing. Jessie gently guides him away, and you travel out of
the canyon.

Complete quest 34. Lose adventure card 41 (Gift’s Map). Gain
