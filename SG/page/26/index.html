51.9
A woman huddles in the corner of a dark cell. Despite her
ragged state, her floral-print dress and lace-up leather shoes
are unmissable.

“Who are you people?” Her face is covered in grit and her
eyes are weary.

“We’re friends of Henrik,” you say. “We’re from San Francisco.”

“Henrik!” She stiffens. “Don’t get me started on that bum,”
says the woman, crossing her arms. “So, you gonna let me out
of here, or what?”

You make a quick escape and head to the streets. You buy
the woman some new clothes and find an inn where she can
get cleaned up.

“My name is Lisa,” she tells you over a plate of hot bread. “I’m
from Oregon, and I traveled half the globe to visit my brothers
in Norway, but Henrik got us all stuck here. Just tell me you
people have a plan.”

“We have a plan,” says Jessie. “Don’t worry. We’ll get you
back home.”

Gain adventure card 77 (Lisa Grant). Draw 1 ability card. Complete
quest 85. Return to the map.

51.1

51.10
You sneak Kresspin into the castle and head to the kitchens.

“Ah! I haven’t been here in years! The queen would come
down to visit me and we’d have lovely conversations,” says
Kresspin.

You follow one of the servants up to a small balcony garden
where the queen sits among the potted pines and mossy
rocks.

“Milda, I’ve returned,” says Kresspin with a flourish.

The queen screams.

“Who are you people? What is that ghoulish thing?”

“Darling! It’s me, Kresspin.”

“Kresspin?” she says, a little calmer.

“Yes! Your dear friend. I’ve been dreaming of seeing you
again, ever since your husband killed me and left me in that
abandoned church. I held tight to this rock, this strange pebble
that keeps me alive, hoping we could be together again.”

The queen’s features soften. “It is you. Oh, you’re just the
same. And yet-“

“I know. I’m hideous now.”

The queen looks at the skeleton for a long time, biting her lip.
“Kresspin,” she says finally, “you must stay here with me. The
king is unbearable. I need someone to talk to. And if anyone
comes around, I suppose we’ll just hide you in the closet.”
She chuckles. “I can get used to you as a skeleton. You aren’t
much to look at anymore, but your voice hasn’t changed.” She
sighs.

The queen gives you a few treasures from her room in thanks
before sending you back to the kitchens.

Complete quest 77. Gain quest 124. Lose adventure card 62
(Kresspin). Gain 2 fire quartz. Draw 1 ability card. Return to the
map.
