4.5
After hours of scraping and digging, you enter the final chamber
of the temple. A statue of a short, round woman with
curly hair stands in the middle of the room, guarding a pool of
water. Tiny clouds of silver light flutter from the glassy surface.
They skim across the walls and up into the woman’s face,
lighting her stony expression with a wry, enchanting glow.

She holds a fishing pole and a sword like frozen metal. The
pole pries easily from her hand, and you cast the line into the
pool. Out jumps a fish made of stone, eyes of diamonds, and
as it touches the ground, it hardens into a lifeless statuette.
You remove one diamond eye and put the fish back into the
pool, where it wriggles away.

Complete quest 4. Gain adventure card 7 (Ice Blade). Gain 1 ice
diamond. Return to the map.
