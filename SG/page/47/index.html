him any more questions.

93.5
A hand shoots up out of the water, clasping the weapon you
threw and quickly dragging it back into the depths, leaving
barely a ripple behind.

A moment later, a woman walks out of the lake, water cascading
down her sodden form. Her dark hair clings like seaweed
to the side of her gray-skinned face. You feel an incredible
sense of monumental age from this woman-like looking at an
ancient statue at a museum, although you wouldn’t dare guess
at the true age of such a water creature. In her right hand, she
carries an ornate sword. The sword gleams in the sun, miraculously
free of rust despite having been stored underwater for
an incalculable amount of time. When the creature speaks,
her voice is cold and airy, like the wind that blows across the
lake.

“Nyn thanks you for your tribute, but if you have come on
behalf of the false king, Nyn warns you thus: Trusiam is no
descendant of Anfortas, and I will not give up my treasure for
the sake of a petty old man desperately clinging to power and
self-importance.”

Nyn grips the sword tighter-a clear warning.

A. Fight her.
(Turn to BOSS 10 on the opposite page.)

B. Trick her.
e cuNNING 11
Fail: Your attempts to confuse her leave you confused. -5 health
and gain madness.
