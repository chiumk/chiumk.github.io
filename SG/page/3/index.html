Mountain winds tickle the ochre grass, brushing the valley
until thousands of stalks sway like waves on a yellow sea.
Meecra’s shrine, a monolith carved with a crescent moon,
casts a long, dark shadow, like a patch of night that has not
yet melted away. A thick cloud gathers around the base of the
boulder, and Meecra strides out.

“How goes your search? We must hurry. Orfash grows stronger the longer we wait.”

A. Ask Meecra to tell you more about totems.
(Turn to 6.1)

B. Ask Meecra where to find totems.
(Turn to 6.2)

C. Leave.
Return to the map.

6.1

6.1
“The power of the totems is linked to the power of the gods.
As the gods have grown weak, the power of the totems has
faded. If you rejuvenate enough of them, I will have the power
to make the Helthark sleep and allow you to go home.”

Meecra picks a dried, red blossom and crushes it in her fingers.
The peppery aroma soon has your eyes watering.

“The burning of this sunrose is strong but momentary. The
power of a totem now fades just as quickly. To return their
power you can use local herbs, though sometimes it takes
more precious ingredients. But there are maps and clues to
help you find them. Here, take this one.”

Gain quest 13.
(Turn to 6)

Narrow boulders wear moss like satin nightgowns, at their
feet a set of gaping caves. You and Jessie decide to take a
look. You catch her as she misses her step on a patch of loose
gravel.

“Thanks, Claire. I’m sure Grandpa would be glad you’re here
watching out for me.”

“That’s a laugh!” you say. “George thought I was irresponsible.”

“No, he always liked you better than me,” Jessie replies. “I
asked him so many times to teach me how to fly. You know
what he said?”

“That it’s too dangerous?”

“That I didn’t have the brains for it,” Jessie kicks a nearby
stone, sending it hurtling down the slope. “And then he taught
you.”
