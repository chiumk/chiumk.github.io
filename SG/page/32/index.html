Quiet grasses poke out of the snow, swaying with the blustering
breath of the mountain. To the north there’s a face
carved in the slope; a crooked nose runs hundreds of feet up
to a smooth eye. In the gap between peaks there’s a swirling,
glowing anomaly, the second eye of the mountain face and
your gateway back to Earth: the God Eye.
