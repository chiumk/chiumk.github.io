72.1
From a fallen bison you collect meat and crystallized minerals
growing on the horns like lichen.

Gain 1 tundra berry, 3 ice diamonds, and 2 meat.

“What trouble did you cause these bison, zarg rat?” says
Ezarius.

The rat-man wears a hurt expression. “You would instantly
frame me for a crime? I should expect nothing else. Zarg rats
have a reputation, you know, though it be unfair. I am no
pillager! Nor are the rats in my village, though they’re treated
like vermin by the canyon miners in Tharkolm.”

Jessie, Miguel, and Ed regard the zarg rat with a mixture of
puzzlement and hesitation.

“I beg your pardon. I only assumed so because the bison were
chasing you. Do you live nearby?” asks Ezarius.

“I would tell you, but how can I trust you? We have been
endlessly hunted and hated. Tharkolm now threatens to seek
out our village and destroy our homes. They say we’re stealing
their mining tools, but we’ve done no such thing! I want to
sell my silks at Stormlock City, but I cannot even travel safely
anymore.”

A. Offer to negotiate for the zarg rats.
