touch it.

“That’s Thrack, god of time and rot,” says Ezarius.

After hesitating a moment, Marani sighs, climbing the statue
and removing two pebbles from the open beak. As she drops
to the floor, a dark miasma seeps from the beak, the putrid
mist pouring down in a slow fall of plague. A ragged, raspy
voice fills the hall.

“Welcome to my chamber, thieves! For trespassing, you will
suffer and rot.”

Withstand the miasma!
