111.4
The historians surrender, muttering promises to hand over the
eye. One of them returns from a tent with a canvas bag, the
bottom crimson, wet, and oozing.

“It never stops bleeding,” says the scholar, tossing it with a
revolted shiver.

When you return to the pyramid and hand over the bag to
the hag, she takes the veiny organ from the canvas bag and
swallows it whole.”

“Can’t use that one anymore, but now it’ll grow back,” she
says. Jessie backs away, her face skewed in disgust.

“My pets will retrieve the totem for you,” says the hag as the
room is filled with the pattering of hundreds of little feet. One
by one, thin, deadly cats appear, their glowing eyes watching
you. One of them drops a rough pebble covered in runes at
your feet.

“You’re welcome back anytime!” says the woman, waving with
massive a hand while brushing her grey hair from her face.

Gain quests 146 and 151. Gain 1 meat, 1 torch, 1 tundra berry, 1
bloodflower, and 1 gergon leaf. Return to the map.
