95.6

95.6
You try to anticipate where the stag would go, but as you walk
deeper and deeper into the woods you can no longer hear the
sounds of the hunt.

You’re standing on the banks of a river, contemplating the
possibility of giving up, when the white stag once again leaps
past you. You give chase again, fording the shallow river, and
force the stag into a ravine, where cliff walls block its path
away from you.

You have it cornered.

Just as you reach for your weapons, the beast transforms
before your eyes, growing in distorted and terrible ways until
it’s barely recognizable. It lets out a deep, guttural cry before
lowering its head and charging at you.

96.3

96.3
“Oh, thank you for the offer! As you may know, at the
Outreach Office we’re primarily focused on recruitment and
procurement, but we also liaise with various allies who are
sympathetic to our cause. I assume you fit into that category?
If so, here.” Bedi hands you a small stack of papers with
detailed instructions printed on them.

“First, we need people to help us spread the word. We’d like
you to pick a town, just one town, which you think might be
sympathetic to our cause and hand out these pamphlets to
the citizens there.”

Miguel is already eagerly reading through one of the pamphlets.

“Second, we’ve been having trouble securing some important
supplies to establish secure trade partnerships. If you could
donate some supplies to our cause, that would be a huge
boost to our functionality. I’ve written down what we need.”

Ed is reading through the list of required supplies with the
eyes of a practiced businessman making value calculations.

“Finally, and I’m only asking you this because you look like
a tough bunch, we’d like you to help get rid of a beast that
roams the tunnels down here. We know it’s controlled by the
king, but no one quite knows what it looks like, since no one
has survived an encounter with it. It’s hindering our ability to
make full use of the secret travel network under the city.”

Jessie volunteers. “Now that sounds like fun!”

Bedi smiles again. “Of course, we’ll pay you what we can for
any work you do for us. Just let me know when you’ve completed
any of these tasks.”

Gain quests 176, 177, 178, and 179.
(Turn to 96.1)
