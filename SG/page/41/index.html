78.1

78.1
You scramble into the canyon and find a narrow cave to hide
in. The horses pound the ground above, and a few of the men
descend on ropes, searching the canyon walls, but they don’t
find you.

“You have no idea what good you’ve done,” says the ragged
man. “My name is Nuruk. I’ve been printing pamphlets and
urging the people of Stormlock City to rebel. We must dethrone
King Trusiam; the man is a corrupt tyrant.”

He gives you a pamphlet and a small red book with his signature
in it. “Use this to get through the secret passage near
Meriok, the mountain village northwest of here. The guards
will open the door for you.”

Gain quest 75. Gain 1 ogroot. Mark 1 box on the Stormlock City
Rebels track. Cross off location 78. Return to the map.

Canvas tents shiver in the cold morning mist. Beside them, a
stream gurgles down a hill of smooth stones. There’s a harkian
in the camp staring at the dead ashes of a fire, mumbling to
himself and shivering.

“Something waits in the cold night. Beyond the veil of this
world. Beyond where the gods may tread. A dark something
watches us and pulls us to endless oblivion. The headless one.
He leads us to the headless one!”

Miguel crouches down next to him. “What does that mean?
The headless one?”

The harkian mumbles to himself, ignoring everything.

80 [rzl
If keyword END, and you have at feast 5 restored totems (9 if playing
a long campaign), and you are ready to finish your campaign,
turn to 120.

Here at the doorstep of the God Eye lies the graveyard of
those claimed by the portal and the Helthark.

Steel bones litter the fields and smoke drifts from the wreckage.
One of the planes has been fashioned into a dwelling.
A wooden wall closes off a section where the steel body has
been ripped in half.

Ed knocks on the door, and a man in a strange mix of clothing-
no doubt scrounged from the crashed planes-answers
with a grin. He tells you he sells the valuables from the
crashed planes.

“The Helthark downed most of these flying machines, but
some crashed on their own. We can transport the fuel to your
craft if you need it. We did that a few times for some other
customers, but the Helthark killed every one of them as soon
as they went near the God Eye.”

A. Buy fuel.
Pay any 3 resources to refuel the plane to full. Return to the map.

B. Scrounge the planes for something useful.
~ SAVVY 14
Fail: -14 health.
(Turn to 99)

C. Leave.
Gain 1 torch. Return to the map.
