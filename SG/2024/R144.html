Map 18 holds all the children’s request: cold symbols for ice cream,
mountain symbols for cake. Map 19 shows, though the mother calls
for cake, one symbol is on her mind and was the only symbol she
didn’t mention out loud. The answer to the riddle is ice cream. If
you ans.wered correctly, gain 1 tundra berry and 1 ice diamond.

If you solved the riddle (or failed to produce the correct answer),
cross off the symbol on map page 19 on the journey log map. You
may not solve this riddle again during the same campaign.
