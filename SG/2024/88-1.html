88.1
“It’s an absolutely magnificent collection,” says Miguel. “But
the artist, Rascan, wants to destroy it. You must save the art
before it’s lost forever!”

“But if the artist wants to destroy it, isn’t that their right?”
asks the art trader.

“Absolutely not. These pieces are moving and surreal. They
have a deep power that could inspire generations of painters.
We can’t let them be lost,” says Miguel.

“And we offer this priceless collection for a mere fraction of
what it’s worth,” says Ed.

The art trader nods. “I suppose you’re right. I’ll get down
there and pick them up as soon as I can.” He gives you a small
pouch filled with crystals.

Complete quest 135. Gain 1 fire quartz and 2 ice diamonds. Cross
off location 88. Cross off location 12 on map 3. Return to the map.
