78 [z}
A ragged man with midnight hair stumbles through the grass,
his desperate gaze resting on you as if he were swimming in
a vast ocean and you were a boat on the horizon. In the far
distance, four men on horses gallop in your direction.

The ragged man calls out as he gets closer. “King Trusiam’s
men are hunting me down for spreading the truth about the
corrupt king! If they catch me, they’ll kill me! Please, help me
escape!”

A. Search for a place to hide in the nearby canyon.
9 PERCEPTION 10
Fail: -12 health. Gain weakened. (As you climb over the canyon rim,
you slip and fall to a rocky ledge.)
(Turn to 78.1)

B. Turn the man over to King Trusiam’s men.
@ STRENGTH 10
Fail: -9 health. (The ragged man fights for his life and bashes you in
the head.)
(Turn to 78.2)

C. Tell the man you can’t help him.
(Turn to 78.3)
