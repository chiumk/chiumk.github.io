50

50~
Clouds blanket the night sky, growling with faraway thunder.

OBSTACLE: Darkness
Lose 1 torch OR take 5 damage and gain frightened.

As you search for shelter from the coming storm, Ed halts,
staring into the darkness ahead. “I thought I saw my wife out
there.” He wanders into the dark mist.

You dash after him, tripping through the tall grass, over roots
and gnarled branches. Suddenly, the mist clears, and you stop
to face three large demons, shiny and black, with mouths of
square, pearly teeth. One of the demons holds Ed by the leg.

“Do you think he’s tasty? I haven’t had a good meal in ages!”

“Yes, I’m so tired of eating squirrels,” says another.

As they lift Ed toward their disgusting mouths, you call out.
“Stop!”

A. Make a deal with the demons to let Ed go.
e cuNNING 11
Fail: You must now choose B.
(Turn to 50.1)

B. Fight the demons!
COMBAT 31, 32, 33 (Level 9)
If you are defeated, cross off location 50 and return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, turn to 50.2)
