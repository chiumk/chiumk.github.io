In the outer wall of the palace, you find the broken iron pipe,
just as the bandits described. The passage inside is dark and
filled with dusty boxes and forgotten junk-broken armor,
old furniture, torn books, and stacks of old clothes. The vault
door bulges like a round, heavy belly about to pop its rusty
rivets. Inside, the bandits are already rummaging through
chests of treasure!

“What the hells are you doing here?” one bandit snaps.

“We’re just the cleaning crew,” says Ed. “We’re here to polish
the silver. Hand me that platter, would you?”
