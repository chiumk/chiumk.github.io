103.2
You find an ancient stairway cut into the rock and follow
it down, where you discover a fountain, empty and lifeless
except for a few piles of dust and a strange piece of armor.
Though dirt and water stains blot the surface, it shines in the
secondhand sunlight.

“Looks like it’s made of glass,” says Ed, picking it up. “But it
weighs almost nothing.”

Gain adventure card 64 (Ghost Bracer).

You wander along a ledge to another ancient structure. The
shattered buildings must have once been magnificent, but
intricate columns and broken statues are all that’s left of a
great palace.

A bit of smoke drifts at the edge of your vision. Is there a fire
nearby?

“Did you see that?” asks Miguel.

“This place is thick with spirits,” says Ezarius, his shoulders
tensed.

More smoke drifts through the hall. It gathers together and
forms into headless people in tattered clothes, ethereal and
flowing as if underwater. Their heads suddenly appear:
cracked, floating insect heads.

“Disgusting,” says Jessie, backing away as the ghosts surround
you.

COMBAT 18, 58, 59, 60 (Level 7)
You may run from this combat at the end of any enemy phase. If
you do, return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, turn to 103.3)
