73.5
You pick up one end of the giant bone and start biting off
hits of meat, chewing and making noises of hungry satisfaction.
“This is delicious!” you say, and continue smacking on
your meal. The giant looks pleased and his face twists into a
grimace you assume is a smile. Then he walks over to the cage
and takes out one of the twins.

“I’m pretty sure we just took a step back,” says Ed, morbidly.
