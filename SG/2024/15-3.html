15.3
The mining guild mercenaries camp in the heart of the canyon,
where ice grips the jagged walls, and every shadow cradles
a deep and healthy frost. Nearby, a giant statue cut into the
shape of a squid looms over the campsite-an unsettling
visage to scare away unwanted visitors. The guild captain
greets you. “Glad you joined us. Don’t mind the hideous
statue-we’re planning to destroy it sometime soon. We were
beginning to worry you wouldn’t show up.”

“The zarg rats are vicious?” asks Jessie.

“Oh, quite nasty. Very dangerous. That’s why we needed as
many mercenaries as possible.”

The guild captain whistles and the miners and mercenaries
advance, circling the statue and walking down a narrow path.

Before long, you emerge into a chamber filled with neat, stone
houses. Fat spiders hang from droopy webs on the ceiling.
Rat-people appear at the doors of the houses, some of them
wielding crossbows and spears, others gripping half-woven
sheets of silk. A panic breaks out at the sight of the mining
guild troops.

“Gather all the spider silk! I don’t want it damaged!” calls the
guild captain.

“Wait, we’re taking their property?” Miguel protests. “These
rats don’t look like a threat to me.”

“Quiet! Everyone fall in line!” shrieks the guild captain. The
miners charge the village.

A. Defend the zarg rats.
COMBAT 23, 40, 41, 42, 43 (Level 8)
You may run from this combat at the end of any enemy phase. If
you do, cross off location 15 and return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, turn to 15.4)

B. Raid the village.
® MOXIE 12
Fail: -10 health.
(Turn to 15.6)

C. Sneak away.
G PERCEPTION 9
Fail: -12 health. (The guild captain finds you sneaking away and
orders his men to attack the traitors!)
(Turn to 15.7)
