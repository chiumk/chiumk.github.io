place an air of magnificence, though their general disrepair
reveals the underlying decay of the city. High above, the
structure extends outward like a table, casting oppressive
shadows on the streets below. Guards in fur hats surround the
only visible entrance-a set of towering double doors.

“This is the palace of King Trusiam,” says a stern guard. “State
your business here or leave.”
