23 [Q]
Miguel a11d Jessie walk ahead, passing spouting geysers encrusted
with sulfur crystals.

“You think I don’t know anything, just because I didn’t go to
all those fancy schools like you?” she asks, perturbed.

“No. I just think you need to open your mind a little. Read
some of the critique and maybe you’ll understand the art
better,” says Miguel.

“I read plenty, and I guarantee I have a more open mind than
you do.”

Miguel sighs. “Did you even go to high school?”

“Aha! See?”

“I just think you haven’t learned enough-“

“You’re digging yourself deeper into that hole, Miguel,” Ed
warns, but Miguel doesn’t listen.

“I suppose I could tutor you,” he muses, “pass along some of
the knowledge I’ve gained.”

“That’s it. Shut your trap and don’t talk to me until I lose the
urge to hit you in the mouth. Tell him to shut up, Claire!”

A. Take your cousin Jessie’s side.
