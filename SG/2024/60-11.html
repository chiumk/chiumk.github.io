Inside, headless statues line the wall, but they haven’t lost
their heads. It looks like they never had any to begin with. A
dark :igure shuffles out of the shadows. Jessie backs away

“George Gift?” says Jessie hesitantly.

He stares at you with wild eyes then, in an instant, seems to
regain his composure.

“Hello friends!” He shares a warm smile and he’s brimming
with charisma. “George Gift is my name,” he states as if Jessie
hadn’t just said it. “Don’t tell me you haven’t seen my movies?
The papers call me ‘A Gift to the cinema!”‘ He laughs heartily
at his own joke and continues, “Of course, you’ve seen them.
Now you remember, don’t you?”
