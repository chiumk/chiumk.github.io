You drop to your stomach, peering over the edge as Jessie
calls out, “Miguel! Are you okay?”

for an hour already.

“Don’t worry. We’ll get you out of there,” says Ed. “Ruby will
never forgive me if I don’t get you home to her.”

Miguel slumps. “I don’t know about that.”
