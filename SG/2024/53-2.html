53.2
“I left her with a good family in Thistletown. I can’t take care
of her anymore,” says Gelca.

Laikeia deflates. “Aunty, I want to go with you.”

“No! You’ve been nothing but trouble to me.”

“You can’t just abandon her!” says Jessie, perturbed.

Gelca snorts, turning on Jessie. “I’m keeping her safe! If she’s
with me, she’ll be in constant danger.”

Ezarius interrupts. “Perhaps we should listen to Gelca. I think
she knows what she’s doing; her work must have great importance.”

A sudden interest fills Miguel’s eyes. “What’s that supposed
to mean, Ezarius?”
