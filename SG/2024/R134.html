134 RIDDLE ROCK ~
Three little children yelling at their mother. “Cake! Cake!”
screamed the youngest, jumping up and down in front of his
mother. “Ice cream! Ice cream!” wailed the middle child, who
draped herself across the floor in white, wet tears. “Why not
both?” yelled the oldest child. “Cake and ice cream. Cake and
ice cream to please us all!” But he was too far from his mother,
and she could not see or hear him. Instead, she opened one
eye and winked the other. Then she declared, “Cake it is. Cake
it will be. Cake for you, and cake for me!” But secretly, there
was only one thing she wanted. What was it?

Attempt to solve the riddle. The answer lies on the open atlas pages.

your journey log and turn to 144. Make sure to only look at paragraph
144, as other riddle answers are nearby. You only have one
try. If incorrect, you cannot try again.
