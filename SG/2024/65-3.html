You walk through the residential district of Stormlock City.
Workers and their families dwell in ancient towers that are
cracked and covered in pipes. Old men sweep their doorways
and children chase cats across the pipes. The smell of roasted
nuts hangs in the air.

A. Eat at a street cart.
Pay any 1 resource.
(Turn to 65.1)

B. Search for Laikeia’s aunt.
Requires keyword AUNTY.
(Turn to 104)

C. Search for Milkarf so you can pay Gloro the Trader’s debt.
Requires keyword DEBT.
(Turn to 105)

D. Visit the alley market.
Requires keyword ART.
(Turn to 65.4)

E. Leave.
Return to the map.
