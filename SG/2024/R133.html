133 RIDDLE ROCK ~
*I was delirious, frozen and frostbitten, stiff with fear and paralyzing
hunger. I could only take one step at a time.

Down, down I went. Down to the mountain range. Right
into danger, right in a valley of snow, I looked up at three icy
fingers. As I gazed, I left my body and went flying through the
clouds.*

Attempt to solve the riddle. The answer lies on the open atlas pages.
Look at the landscape. Write your answer as a description of
the number(s) and/or symbol(s) in your chosen atlas square. Write
this on your journey log and turn to 143. Make sure to only look at
paragraph 143, as other riddle answers are nearby. You only have
one try. If incorrect, you cannot try again.
