40.13 ~
A poster written in lavish script reads:

“King Trusiam seeks brave adventurers to recover art if acts related
to his famed ancestor, King Anfortas of Old Cadwahlyn.
Lavish rewards will be granted in exchange for these artifacts.”

The rest of the poster contains dense information on the possible
locations of three different artifacts. Miguel immediately
starts to copy it all down into his notebook.

“Aspects of these stories are reminiscent of Arthurian legends.”
Miguel says, his voice eager. “How fascinating! I wonder
if the stories originated here and somehow made it over
to our world in altered form, or perhaps vice versa? Or is it all
a coincidence? Another aspect of the collective unconscious,
powerful enough to transcend the barriers between worlds?”

“Ugh, who cares?” Jessie tears the poster off the wall. “There,
just fold that up and bring it with us. Much easier. And you
can study it as much as you want.”

Complete quest 76. Gain quests 166, 172, and 201.
(Turn to 40)
