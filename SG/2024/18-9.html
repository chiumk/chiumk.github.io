18.9
Eedarah lies in the ice, coughing through reddened lips. Blood
runs from her mouth down her chin, mixing into a gash made
by one of Thrack’s thick talons.

“This is the end for me. I die knowing there’s a little less evil in
the world.”

“Nol You will not die!” In a flash of feathers, Hessrah turns
into a giant falcon and grips Eedarah in her talons, flapping
through the cave toward the surface. “I will take her to my
father Kemter! He will heal her!” she calls out.

“I couldn’t stand it if she dies!” says Jessie, her forehead
creased in worry.

“Kemter is the god of growth and plants. Perhaps he will heal
her, though I hold little hope. The gods are treacherous,” says
Ezarius.

Gain adventure cards 89 (Thrack’s Heart) and 90 (Thrack’s Brain).
Complete quests 53 and 78. Lose adventure cards 51 (Eedarah)
and 88 (Hessrah). Return to the map.
