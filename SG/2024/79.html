stream gurgles down a hill of smooth stones. There’s a harkian
in the camp staring at the dead ashes of a fire, mumbling to

world. Beyond where the gods may tread. A dark something
watches us and pulls us to endless oblivion. The headless one.
He leads us to the headless one!”

Miguel crouches down next to him. “What does that mean?
The headless one?”

The harkian mumbles to himself, ignoring everything.
