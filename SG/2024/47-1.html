47.1
Gloro is delighted! “How did you find this?” He cradles the
book of sketches like it’s a newborn.

“We found it to the west of here and recognized the sketch of
this place,” says Jessie.

“I lost this months ago. It was my granddaughter’s sketchbook.
She used to live here, before her parents took her away.
I haven’t seen her for years.” Tears brim over his cheeks.

“Where is she now?” you ask.

“Back in Zokmere,” Gloro scowls at the name. “But I can’t
see her anymore. This sketchbook gives me back a little bit of
her, so I must thank you. You folks are somewhat trustworthy.
Could I hire you to deliver something for me? I owe a significant
debt to a ruthless creditor, but I must stay here with my
trading post or some looter will take everything I have.”

A. Agree to the delivery.
(Turn to 47.2)

B. Decline.
Complete quest 33. Gain 1 tundra berry.
(Turn to 47)
