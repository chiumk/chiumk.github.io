84.1

84.1
Marani whistles, calling for everyone to depart. She guides
you to an intimidating doorway cut into the rock, a slit of midnight
in the slate walls. You follow her through to a chamber,
the walls covered in carvings of hourglasses and skulls. Stairs
as wide as a railroad car lead downward into the dark.

OBSTACLE: Darkness
Lose 1 torch OR take 13 damage and gain frightened.

Below, a horrid stench fills a hall covered in half-buried
bones. The floor is slick with muck and decay. Jessie covers
her mouth. Miguel clamps his shut. As the crew reaches the
center of the chamber, kicking a path through the bones,
unearthly screeches echo from nooks along the wall. Vicious,
black bird demons swoop down, surrounding you.

Fight the bird demons!
COMBAT 19, 20, 21 (Level 9)
You may run from this combat at the end of any enemy phase. If
you do, cross off location 84 and return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, turn to 84.3)
