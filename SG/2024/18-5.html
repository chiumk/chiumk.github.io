18.5
A dark entry stands open in the largest building, the stone
doors swung wide, flattening the snow. Feathers litter the
steps that lead down into shadow.

OBSTACLE: Darkness
Lose 1 torch OR gain frightened and low morale.

The crunch of wet gravel accompanies each step as you trace
the deepening stairway. The last step casts you into a great
hall, high and wide and wintry, as if every year, winter itself
was breathed from its chest. And there in the snowy depths:
a city of glassed water. Its towering ceiling of thick ice morphs
the sunlight into a glow as blue as the arctic sea. It trickles
down the frozen walls, and adorns the buildings with jewels of
shining ice.

As you walk a snowy street, the air turns foul like rotting flesh.
A sickening assault on the otherwise pristine setting. A loud
caw echoes from above. Giant crows, their eyes crusted with
bloody flakes, swoop down and surround you.

COMBAT 19, 20 (Level 6)
You may run from this combat at the end of any enemy phase. If
you do, complete quest 53, lose adventure cards 51 (Eedarah) and
88 (Hessrah), and return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, gain 2 meat and turn to 18.6)
