47.2
Gloro hands you a bundle with a diamond and a stunning,
green egg carefully wrapped inside. “Now don’t lose these, or
I’ll hunt you down!”

Miguel laughs nervously.

“It wasn’t a joke,” says Gloro, staring you down with a pulsing
eye. “You can deliver the diamond and the egg to a man
named Milkarf. He’s a merchant in Stormlock City. And you
can keep this as payment.” He gives you a rare flower.

Complete quest 33. Gain quest 46. Gain 1 blood flower, 1 ice diamond,
and 1 jadeworm egg.
(Turn to 47)
