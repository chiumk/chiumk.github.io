135 ~
The Helthark has found you! You must escape or crash.
Fly your plane through the treacherous mountains and
outmaneuver the pursuing Helthark.

AIR CHASE MIMI GAME

To set up, place the airplane token and the Helthark
token on the start space. To win, you must reach the
last space on the track. You immediately lose if you ever
reach 0 plane health. Place a damage token on the “+0”
on the Helthark power track. Place a damage token on
the 5 on the plane health track.

Each turn, draw 1 ability card for the Helthark, then draw
1 ability card for the plane. Repeat this until you win or
lose.

When you draw a card for the Helthark, you must place
it under one of the 3 Helthark action spaces ( the first 3
spaces on the left side).

When you draw a card for the plane, you must place it
under one of the 3 plane action spaces (the 3 spaces on
the right side).

Each action automatically activates when certain conditions
are met. Only look at the fate number on the ability
cards. When an action activates, perform the action and
discard all ability cards under the action.

Helthark First Action: This activates when there are 2
pair of matching fate numbers or 3 of the same fate number.
This space may never contain more than 2 different
number values. For example, if you have already placed
a 3 and a 5, then you may only place 3s and 5s until the
action activates and all cards are discarded.

If you place 2 pair, move the Helthark back 1 space on
the track. If 3 of a kind, move the token on the Helthark
power track 1 space to the right, then move the Helthark
2 spaces forward. If at any time the Helthark is on the
same space as the plane, deal 1 damage to the plane for
each move the Helthark has left. If you can do none of
these things, simply discard the cards but take no action.

Helthark Second Action: This activates when there
are fate numbers with a sum of 8 or greater. Move the
Helthark token to the plane’s current space, then deal 1
damage to the plane.

Helthark Third Action: This activates when there are
fate numbers with a sum of 12 of greater. Move the Helthark
3 spaces. If at any time the Helthark is on the same
space as the plane, deal 1 damage to the plane for each
move the Helthark has left.

Plane First Action: This activates if there are 2 cards
with a sum less than 8. Move the plane 1 space. If the
sum of fate numbers is ever 8 or greater, it is a bust and
the cards are discarded and no action is taken.

Plane Second Action: This activates if there are 3 cards
with a sum less than 11 or greater than 16. Move the
plane 2 spaces. If the sum of fate numbers is from 11 to
16, it is a bust and you must discard all cards here and
take no action.

Plane Third Action: This activates if there are 4 cards
with consecutive fate numbers (for example, 4,5,6,7).
They MUST BE PLACED consecutively in ascending
order (for example, if the current highest fate here is 4,
you must place a 5 next; you may not place a 6 until you
have first placed a 5). Move the plane 3 spaces.

Helthark Power: Each time the Helthark deals damage,
it deals extra damage based on its power. For example, if
the Helthark activated its third action and moved 1 space
to the plane, it would then attack twice (1 attack for each
remaining move). Each attack deals 1 damage normally,
but if the Helthark had + 2 power, it would deal 3 damage
each attack for a total of 6!

Helthark Movement: The Helthark never moves past the
plane. If it is on the same space as the plane and moves,
it instead deals 1 plane damage per Helthark movement
until the plane loses all health or it escapes.
