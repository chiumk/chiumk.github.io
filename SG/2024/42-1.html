42.1
A white citadel stands in the snow, its seamless walls insurmountable.

“The Pann traveler said we had to spill blood on the tower wall
to enter,” says Miguel.

“Does that mean a whole lot of blood? Or just a drop? How
does a tower know?” says Jessie, eyeing the ivory walls warily.

“Let’s start with just a little,” says Ezarius, slicing his hand with
a knife. He presses the blood on the tower wall. The blood
spreads, flowing into the shape of a door handle. You pull it
open and find a passageway.

Miguel blinks at the crimson handle, leaning down to take a
closer look. “Fascinating.”

“I’m not touching that,” says Jessie.

A spiral passage of marble stairs leads upwards. You climb
until your legs burn and your lungs demand a rest. You don’t
have much time to catch your breath, as a hoard of strange
eyeballs and sneering demons approaches.

COMBAT 10, 11, 31, 34, 35 (Level 9)
You may run from this combat at the end of any enemy phase. If
you do, return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, turn to 42.2)
