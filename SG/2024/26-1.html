26.1
The case is filled with pale dresses, notebooks jotted with
neat handwriting, books, and a beach hat.

“This belongs to a Mrs. Flora Snyder,” says Miguel. “And she’s
an author. Look at these notebooks filled with prose.” He
reads aloud.

Under mean skies the kindest of flowers taunt me sweetly.

‘Stay, stay,’ they whisper. ‘What can your home offer that
we cannot provide?’

My wistful heart replies, ‘Neither here nor home can provide
me freedom, strange blossom.’

‘Come, come,’ they whisper. ‘We can provide. We can
provide.’

Miguel takes out a pressed flower, the petals still soft like
satin. “She could still be close by.”

Jessie calls out. “Flora!”

A muffled voice calls back. “Here! I’m here!”

You follow the sound to a soggy pit by the ridge. A spectacled
woman in a torn dress sits forlornly at the bottom. Ezarius
reaches down a large hand and pulls her up.

“Oh, thank goodness. Who are you people? How did you
know I was here?”

“We found your luggage,” says Miguel, offering the muddy
case.

She reaches for it, then hesitates. “My husband is here too.
He wandered over here after we had an argument. I was looking
for him when I fell into the pit.”

“I’m sure he’s somewhere around here. We’ll help you find
him,” says Ezarius.

Gain quest 48. Gain adventure card 49 (Flora Snyder). Cross off
location 26. Return to the map.
