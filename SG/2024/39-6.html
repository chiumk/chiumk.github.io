39.6
The woman approaches you. “I suppose I should thank you
for dispatching those monsters. But what were you doing near
my camp?”

“Trying to distract them,” says Ed smoothly.

“The curses of Thrack haunt this place. I was following the
putrid god and those monsters infested with his vile disease.
He’s heading northeast, hellsbent on finding a frozen city.”

“Do you know what he wants in the city?” asks Ezarius.

“I do not. But I can no longer search for him myself. Perhaps
you would travel with me? My name is Eedarah,” she says. “I
plan to destroy Thrack. He’s in a weakened state-all the gods
are, so we have a chance.”

A. Agree to hunt the god Thrack.
(Turn to 39.4)

B. Tell the woman you’re busy trying to get home.
(Turn to 39.5)
