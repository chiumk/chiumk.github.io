96.11
You’re not sure how to tell if a zarg rat is smiling, but Lug
seems pleased.

“Thank you for this. King Trusiam keeps throwing people in
the dungeon for the most minor infractions, so it’s hard to
maintain a stable trade network. But with this to start with we
should be able to trade more reliably. We can only give you
this for now, but if you visit again in the future, we should
have more resources available to trade.”

Lose quest 178. Gain quest 184. Gain 1 tundra berry, 1 ice diamond,
and 1 jadeworm egg. Mark 1 box on the Stormlock City Rebels
track.
