10.4
The huge eyes roll upward again, the rough rock grating on
itself and spewing small clouds of white dust.

“That’s a being of living stone, created by Shorme, goddess of
stone and the fire of the deep earth,” says Ezarius. “You were
wise to leave. Shorme is a merciless god-as untrustworthy as
those who follow her.”

“Do you trust Meecra?” you ask, but Ezarius doesn’t reply.
He’s down at the river reaching for the leather bag you saw
earlier. It bobs in the current and disappears into the gaping
mouth before he can snatch it.

Gain 1 ogroot and 2 rope. Cross off location 10. Return to the map.
