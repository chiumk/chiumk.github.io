40.10
“A favor?” says one of the guards, smiling. “We need help
taking out a rebel hideout. Those scum hide in the abandoned
parts of the city.”

The husks of dead pipes, half-rusted into oblivion, line the
empty streets as the guards lead you north. Turning into an
alley, you navigate broken cobblestones and collapsed walls
until you see strands of light escaping a barred window.

“Bust in there and take them out. We’ll arrest any after they
surrender,” says the guard. The other guards chuckle.

“We shouldn’t be doing their dirty work,” mumbles Miguel.

A. Fight the guards.
COMBAT 42, 43, 50, 51 (Level 9)
You may run from this combat at the end of any enemy phase. If
you do, gain quest 101. Cross off location 40. Return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, turn to 40.11)

B. Capture the rebels in their hideout.
~ STRENGTH 14
Fail: -15 health. Gain weakened.
(Turn to 40.12)
