“Help me with supper, and I will tell you what I know.” Henrik
hands you a smooth clay pot of dried herbs. “They all split
up as soon as we met Meecra. Some went looking for totems.
A few of us stayed here to wait for the other passengers to
return.”

“How many passengers were there?” you ask.

“There were twenty when we landed, but not all are still alive.
The twins and I were waiting here for the others to return with
enough totems for Meecra, but now that we have you to help
us, our chances are much greater.”

“Twins?” asks Ed. “I didn’t see anyone else around.”

“Yes, there was a young woman and her brother. They went to
the lake to catch fish, but I haven’t seen them in days.”

Gain quest 5. Gain adventure card 28 (Roast Meat).
