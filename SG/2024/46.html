46~
A bearded man beckons you inside. “This used to be a
temple to Shorme, godpess of rock and fire. But now it’s my
workshop. I study artifacts from the old Pann empire. Before
the gods destroyed it, the empire stretched all the way from
Alzarria to the northern tundra.” The man turns to Ezarius.
“You’re a Lukran, aren’t you? It seems Lukra is to share the
same fate as Pann.”

Ezarius clenches his fists. “Yes. I was there when Lukra City
was destroyed by Shorme.”

“Sorry to hear that. At least, if the rumors are true, the gods
have lost much of their power thanks to a group called the
Red Heretics. Let’s hope it’s permanent, eh?”

“Let’s hope,” says Ezarius quietly.

A. Enter the workshop.
(Turn to 46.3)

B. Ask the man to examine the metal box you found.
Requires keyword BOX.
(Turn to 46.1)

C. Share a drink.
Gain 1 whiskey. Return to the map.
