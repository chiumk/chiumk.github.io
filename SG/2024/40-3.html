40.3
“You’re an ally of the king,” sneers one of the rebels.

“If you dethrone the king, what then? It’s likely you’ll replace
him with more of the same,” says Ed.

“We just don’t want this old man getting hurt,” says Jessie,
lifting her bat.

The rebels take your meaning and disappear into the dark,
abandoned streets to the north.

The old man thanks you and gives you a small gift.

Gain quest 89. Mark 1 box on the King Trusiam track. Cross off
location 40. Return to the map.
