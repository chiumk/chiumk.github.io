123 END~
You fasten the three pieces of the spear together. Purple energy
swirls from the stone and surrounds the weapon.

“We have the spear! We can destroy the Helthark if we want
to. We don’t need Meecra or the totems anymore,” says
Jessie.

As you hold the glowing weapon, a sudden darkness gathers
around you. Two moonlight eyes emerge from a thickening
cloud, which clears to reveal Meecra.

“This could have devastating effects on our worlds,” she says.
“If you kill the Helthark, I fear the God Eye would close before
you are ready. The God Eye and the Helthark are intrinsically
linked.”

“How do we know for sure? Of course you don’t want us to
use the spear,” says Ed. “You want the totems.”

Meecra folds her arms. “I believe I can use the power of the
spear, instead of the totems. And we won’t need to kill the
Helthark.”

Move the Claire token immediately to location 80 on map 13, then
turn to 123.1.
