E122.2

122.2 END
You leave Ezarius, hoping Meecra will save him and climb
aboard the plane.

The plane shudders as it releases its grip on the yellow grass
and soars toward the giant face in the mountain and the God
Eye. The portal flickers as you grow closer, sputtering with the
last moments of its life.

A sudden screech shakes the plane and pierces your ears like
a knife. The Helthark’s titanic shadow climbs up the mountain
face; you can see it above you. It swoops down, eager to dash
your plane against the cliffs.

An eerie song drifts over the mountain. The Helthark slows,
flapping its wings laboriously. It drifts to the ground with a
massive thump, asleep, just as you fly through the swirling
mass of the God Eye. Home waits for you on the other side.
You can feel your heart racing. Have you finally returned?

Clouds surround the plane. In a gap ahead, something tall and
majestic and red stands above a glittering sea; the Golden
Gate Bridge welcomes you home.

“We’re back!” Jessie calls, and a cheer erupts in the back of
the plane.

Miguel radios the airstrip, and by the time you land, Ruby,
Ed’s daughter, is waiting with teary eyes. She runs to Ed and
throws her arms around his neck, then dashes to Miguel,
covering him with kisses. You watch the sun dip below the far
horizon of the ocean, thinking about Ezarius, hoping he made
it, though you’ll never know for sure.
