89.1
As the sun sets, the empty mansion comes to life. Lanterns
glow in the windows. Townsfolk and travelers approach the
door, where they either hand over a paper invitation or pay a
fee to gain entrance. You stop a woman in a red dress and a
large hat and ask her what’s going on.

“Oh, it’s an art show!” The woman radiates eagerness and
excitement. “I’ve heard they’re displaying art from another
world, too! I’m nowhere near rich enough to buy any of it,
but they’re letting anyone in with just a small donation to the
Milius Miners and Merchants Concern. I simply couldn’t miss
the opportunity!”

Ed frowns as the woman hurries away. “The sheer indignity of
being asked to pay to look at my own collection. I can’t bear
it!”

A. Line up and pay the fee.
Lose 1 rare resource or 3 common resources.
(Turn to 89.4)

B. Sneak around the back.
G PERCEPTION 9
Fail: You trip into a briar patch. -3 health
(Turn to 89.5)
