28 [Q]
If keyword HAUNTING, turn to 28.3

Cold wind blasts through the canyon from the vast tundra to
the north.

OBSTACLE: Cold
You may play an adventure card that ignores cold OR take 4 damage
and gain weakened.

Strange figures climb down from above - white, hairy beasts
with homs.

“Leave us alone! We’re not good for eating!” yells Jessie,
swinging her baseball bat.

If keyword GLUTTON, and you want to attempt to feed the beasts
to the crystal demons you’ve been carrying around, you may turn to
28.2.

COMBAT 45, 46 (Level 7)
You may run from this combat at the end of any enemy phase. If
you do, return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, turn to 28.1)
