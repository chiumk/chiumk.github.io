14 [QI
If keyword ROBBER, turn to 14.4.

A clump of clay chimneys pours a stream of soot and smoke
into the clouds, swirling together like coffee in milk. Workers
in leather aprons push wheelbarrows brimming with ore. A
ragged old man with leaden eyes watches you from within the
shadows of a chalky house.

“You’ve business at Locifa Ironworks?” the man asks. “Korman
is my name. Here we work with more than metal. Compounds
to awaken the old spirits. Potions to revive the powers of
ancient stone.”

A. Ask to see what he can make.
(Turn to 14.1)

B. Ask Korman about himself.
(Turn to 14.2)

C. Mention that you met Goff.
Requires keyword HERMIT.
(Turn to 14.3)

D. Ask if Rascan the painter is here.
Requires keyword IMAGERY.
(Turn to 14.5)

E. Bid Korman farewell.
Return to the map.
