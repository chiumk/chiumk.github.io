If playing normal mode, restore 15 health now. If playing easy mode,
restore all health and remove all status effects now.

A trail of slimy feathers harboring s9uirming maggot stowaways
leads you through the frosted city into an amphitheater.
A massive crow, the god Thrack, lurks in the center,
shuffling behind folded wings and eyeing you with malice. You
fight back a wave of nausea at the rotting carcass stench as
the demonic creature unfolds itself.

Eedarah draws an arrow to her longbow. “We must kill the
god now, before he gains any more strength.”

Thrack cackles and you notice that the bird is holding a basalt
rock close to its chest. A strange energy surrounds it.

If keyword FEATHERS, turn to 18.7. Otherwise, keep reading.

“You will be the first to rot in my new reign.” He hacks and
coughs, and his small, red eyes seem to dig into the deepest
recesses of your mind. Black ooze seeps from his open beak,
dripping onto the frozen ground.
