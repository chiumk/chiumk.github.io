65.7
You give Thadri an emotional plea about the stolen paintings
and the sentimental value of the collection. Thadri seems
unimpressed.

“Look, I don’t ask where my suppliers get the stuff, I just sell it
on at reasonable prices. If you’re going to kick up a fuss about
theft and legal ownership, I may have to call over a few of my
friends to show you out of the market.”

Thadri makes a gesture, and a few nearby toughs take a few
steps closer.

“Please, Thadri,” interjects Ed. “I know what it’s like to run a
business dealing in products that occasionally have dubious
origins. We’re not here to make trouble for you. But you must
also know what it’s like to have a few items you truly could
not bear to part with, under any circumstances-personal
treasures that transcend business concerns. I’m only asking for
your help to retrieve one of my personal treasures.”

Miguel looks at Ed, his brow furrowed.

Thadri sighs. “Fine, I got the pretty picture off a man named
Tekla. Real squirmy fellow, but I’ve had dealings with him
before and he always delivers what he says he will. When I
bought this off him, he was bragging about all the art he’d
recently found. Said he was selling off some of the pieces at a
gallery show near Milius. Art collectors from the south travel
hundreds of miles just to buy art there.”

Ed watches Thadri’s face carefully. “This Tekla, did you see if
he had a piece called Woman with Hairpin with him?”

Miguel gasps. “You were planning to sell Woman with Hairpin?
Why didn’t you tell me? You know how important that
painting is to Ruby.”

“I regret it now,” snaps Ed. “That’s why I need to get it back.”

Thadri glares as them both. “I don’t know any specifics. If you
want to know more, you’ll have to ask Tekla. Here, a small gift
so you’ll remember Thadri’s fondly, and to make up for any
trouble my suppliers caused you. Please remember my name
if you plan on purchasing any more miscellaneous items, and
please forget my name if you plan on speaking to any city
guards about alleged thefts.”

Lose quest 156. Gain quest 160. Gain 1 fire quartz. Return to the
map.
