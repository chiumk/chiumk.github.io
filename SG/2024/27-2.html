27.2
In the deep woods, you find the camp where lmirian’s brother
told the conspirators to meet. Nervous harkians exchange
uncomfortable glances when you arrive.

“It’s well known that most don’t keep the speech rule. But
Ekeren would have us following his every command!”

“But he has too many with him. Many will die if we fight,” says
lmirian.

Horse cries shatter the circle of conspirators; Ekeren and
his followers surround you with sharp sickles and gleaming
spears.

“They must have followed us here!” says lmirian, snatching a
broadsword from the ground.

COMBAT 1, 2, 3, 4, 44 (Level 8)
If you are defeated, lose quest 32. Lose adventure card 37
(lmirian). Gain quest 35. Cross off location 27. Return to the map.
(If you defeat the enemies, continue reading.)

WIN : continue

With hell in his eyes, Ekeren leaps to deal a death blow to
lmirian, casting a bloody spear at him. lmirian spins around
and leaps out of the way, then Ekeren tumbles forward, falling
into a jagged trident. He struggles for breath, choking, reaching
upward. Then he’s still.

“Thank you for helping us. This was a gruesome way to end
things, but I believe we’ll have a free society now that we can
do away with the old laws,” says lmirian.

“Careful you do not abandon all your customs or you’ll find
yourselves lost in a stormy sea of ideas,” says Ezarius.

“Perhaps we will find the library one day,” says lmirian. “Here,
as promised for helping us.”

Complete quest 37. Gain quest 23. Lose adventure card 37
(lmirian). Gain 3 tundra berries and 1 jadeworm egg. Return to the
map.
