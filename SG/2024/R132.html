132 RIDDLE ROCK ~
At our lowest point we found the diamond. It was just as high
as it was cold. And the view of death made us shiver, until we
turned around and saw deliverance.

Attempt to solve the riddle. The answer lies on the open atlas
pages. Look at the landscape. Write your answer as a description
of all numbers and symbols on your chosen atlas square. Write
this on your journey log and turn to 142. Make sure to only look at
paragraph 142, as other riddle answers are nearby. You only have
one try. If incorrect, you cannot try again.
