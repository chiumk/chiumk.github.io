95.4
The hunting party is loud enough that it’s easy to follow their
path through the woods. When you finally catch up to them,
they’ve stopped in another clearing where they’ve set up a
massive cloth pavilion. All of the ghosts you saw before are
there, sharing a mighty feast.

Sitting at a raised table across the pavilion, you can see a flaxen-
haired young man with a wide grin and a jeweled crown on
his head.

“What a splendid hunt that was!” Ed’s voice sounds strange
again.

Miguel claps him on the back. “It’s a shame that gods-forsaken
stag got away from us again, but we come to these hunts for
the food, don’t we?”

They both laugh again. This time, Ezarius joins in the laughter.

“We must be grateful to the gods for sending us such a
splendid king as Anfortas,11 says Ezarius. “He may be young
and inexperienced, but I can tell that over time he will perform
many grand deeds!”

Jessie looks scared now. “Oh no, I think they really have been
possessed!”

Miguel seems to break free of the ghosts’ control for a moment,
just long enough to shout, “Help us!”
