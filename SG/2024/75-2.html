75.2
You show the guards the book with Nuruk’s signature.

“Allies are in short supply,” one says. “Many who would have
fought with us abandoned hope after one of our best warriors
was captured. She’s still stuck in the dungeons.”

“Or dead,” says the other guard.

The first guard scowls. “I think she’s alive, and if you lot can
break her out, we might have a chance again.”

“I think we ought to know why first,” says Miguel. “Are you
sure dethroning the king is the best option? Revolutions bring
only blood and chaos.”

“I thought you were an ally,” says the guard with a raised
eyebrow.

“We’re on your side,” says Jessie. “We just want to know what
we’re getting into. We’re new around here.”

“This isn’t about politics or power. King Trusiam believes he
descends from ancient kings of old, and he’s grown obsessed.
He jails anyone he doesn’t like, and he’s blind to the needs of
the city. Food shortages. High taxes. No one can live like this.”

“Then why do they?” asks Ed. “Where is his support?”

“He won’t have any when we’ve succeeded,” says the first
guard. “This passage leads into Stormlock City. It’s how we’ve
managed to get supplies and spies in and out of the city.”

Complete quest 75. Gain quest 84. Mark 1 box on the Stormlock
City Rebels track.

A. Follow the passage into the city.
(Turn to 75.5)

B. Bid-farewell and return to the entrance.
Gain 1 whiskey. Return to the map.
