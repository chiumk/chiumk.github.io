E121.1

121.1 END
You take off and try to fly to the portal. The god eye is ahead.
You’re almost there! You’re going to make it!

A meteoric screech rattles the plane and shakes the mountains.
The Helthark throws your plane against the mountainside
and it explodes. Everything you know disappears in a
flash of white light.

You awaken on a flat, sandy plane. The sky is filled with stars.
Ghostly beings walk along the desert toward a tunnel of light
on the far horizon.

“Where are they going?” you ask a nearby spirit.

“To eternity. It’s a long, long walk,” they say.

“Then I’d better get started,” you reply, starting the long walk
through the wasteland.

ENDING 2

See rulebook page 29 to calculate your final score and mark your
achievements.

If you have more boxes checked on the King Trusiam track than
the Stormlock City Rebels track, read 124. If you have more boxes
checked on the Stormlock City Rebels track than the King Trusiam
track, read 125. If both tracks have no checked boxes, or they have
an equal number of checked boxes, there is no effect.
