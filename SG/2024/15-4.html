15.4
If keyword DIPLOMAT or PILLAGE, turn to 15.5.

You defeat the raiding miners and they retreat to their encampment.
The zarg rats watch with grateful eyes as one of
them approaches you.

“They’ll think twice about attacking us again, and next time
we’ll be better prepared. They’ve blamed us for stealing their
tools. And yes, some of our number have stolen equipment in
the past, but all the captain really wanted was our spider silk.”

The rats gift you a few rare trinkets.

Complete quest 72. Gain quests 73 and 137. Gain 2 meat, 1 jadeworm
egg, and 2 tundra berries. Return to the map.
